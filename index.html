Brews

Cafe Noir

A fancy-pants (read: French) way of saying black coffee. This classic favorite is simple and effective. Made with ground coffee beans steeped in hot water, this simple drink can be served with cream or sugar mixed in.

Espresso

Imagine cafe noir took it's happy pills this morning. This is what you get! A strong blend taken in smaller doses.

Latte

With espresso as a base, this popular coffee option has a layer of steamed milk with a crown of foam at the top. Commonly mixed with many different flavors and syrups.

Try These Famous Latte Varieties

Cappuccino

Like a latte, but with a greater foam-to-milk ratio. Frequently seasoned with powdered cinammon on top, or mixed with other popular flavors, referred to in the coffee world as "shots".

Here Are Our Favorite Cappuccino Recipes

Americano

The reigning king of power-up beverages, this drink is nothing more than pure espresso diluted with steaming water. For more of a kick, increase the ratio of espresso to water.

Macchiato

A perfect blend between a straight shot of espresso and a cappuccino. This crowd favorite in its simplest incarnation is an espresso base with a dash of milk for flavor, but with many options for variation with appropriate flavor shots.
